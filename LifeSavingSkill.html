CPR

CPR stands for cardiopulmonary resuscitation. It is a life-saving technique used to revive someone who has stopped breathing or whose heart has stopped beating. CPR involves chest compressions and rescue breaths.

Step Description
1 Check for responsiveness
2 Call for help
3 Perform chest compressions
4 Provide rescue breaths

Heimlich Maneuver

The Heimlich Maneuver is a life-saving technique used to help someone who is choking. It involves applying pressure to the abdomen to dislodge the object blocking the airway.

Step Description
1 Stand behind the person
2 Make a fist with one hand and place it above the navel
3 Grasp the fist with the other hand and press inward and upward
4 Repeat until the object is dislodged

Bleeding Control

If someone is bleeding heavily, it is important to control the bleeding as quickly as possible. Here are the steps to take:

Step Description
1 Apply direct pressure to the wound with a clean cloth or bandage
2 Elevate the injured area above the heart, if possible
3 Apply a pressure bandage or tourniquet, if necessary
